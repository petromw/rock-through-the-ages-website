A brief description of me and this website

My name is Mark Petro. I am a student in the Information Technology program at the University of Cincinnati on the software development track. This is a website I designed for my 'Fundamentals of Web Development' course. The purpose of this site is to demonstrate my skills with basic web development.

I have always had a passion for music even though I possess no musical talent of my own. This is why I love learning about artists and music. One of my favorite genres is classic rock. Specifically, I love music from the 70s the most. I wanted to create a website that would give me the opportunity to learn more about rock music. While developing this page, I was able to build on the knowledge I already had about the history of classic rock and the various artists. I included some of my personal opinions where I couldn't find statistics.
